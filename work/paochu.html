overview
Current academic support is often seen as inconvenient and unreliable, discouraging already busy college students from seeking help. Students feel the need to seek help often when it's too late. Thus, I set out to develop a prototype of a product that addresses the urgent academic needs of USC students.
challenge

What are we trying to solve?

How might we design a typeface that is quirky, cute, and inspired by Chinese calligraphy?
project scope

Combining Chinese calligraphy with Roman

Balancing elements of a calligraphy brush with legibility was crucial in designing this typeface. Using Cooper Black as the base, I modified the legs and feet to be distinctly Chinese while adapting the stems to parallel each other.
