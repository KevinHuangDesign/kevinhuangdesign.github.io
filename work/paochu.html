Paochu

A Typeface Inspired by My Mother
project info
when
spring 2020
overview
I was assigned to create my own typeface through fontself. As a second generation Asian American, I struggle to find my identity. My goals were to reflect this internal clash between cultures throughout the typeface. I designed this typeface in honor of my mother, who's name and personality represents a precious, or little, piggy.
challenge

What are we trying to create?

How might we design a typeface that is quirky, cute, and inspired by Chinese calligraphy?
project scope

Combining Chinese calligraphy with Roman

Balancing elements of a calligraphy brush with legibility was crucial in designing this typeface. Using Cooper Black as the base, I modified the legs and feet to be distinctly Chinese while adapting the stems to parallel each other.
