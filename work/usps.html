Satirical Stamps to Save the Postal Service
project info
when
fall 2020
overview
Tasked with creating a suite of images that evoke a response, I drew attention to the current United States Postal Service situation. I aimed to spark conversations about the need to keep mail as a public service, with the goal of arousing anger and support in these turbulent times.

Fuck DeJoy

Appointed Postmaster General DeJoy has earned millions from his financial ties to the postal service. He is responsible for executing delays to the timely service of the USPS, which rural farmers depend upon to transport baby chicks within a day. Millions of baby chickens have died.

Vote Early

Trump threatens our right to vote and democracy by creating delays to the postal service and spreading misinformation about the validity of mail-in ballots. This design draws the connection between voting by mail early to preserving peace and democracy.

Buy Stamps

The USPS is the only service able to deliver mail to every single resident in America, including the most rural of areas. By denying the USPS the funds it needs and pushing to privatize mail, these residents can be left stranded. I used themes of family and human interest to draw empathy from viewers and call them to action.
